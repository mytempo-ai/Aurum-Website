THE VENUE

A Space Born to Celebrate

Where Every Event
Becomes Legendary

Built nearly 150 years ago and reborn after two years of breathtaking renovation, Aurum Events is Freehold's most unique event destination. Step inside and discover a trendy NYC loft — exposed brick, LED-lit walls, recycled wood shelving — right in the heart of downtown Freehold. If you can dream it, we can do it.

  • Main BallroomFull-scale hall with state-of-the-art LED lighting & sound
  • The LoftUnique intimate loft space inside the main ballroom
  • Cocktail PatioOutdoor patio perfect for cocktail hour
  • Rooftop Deck400 sq ft deck overlooking downtown Freehold
